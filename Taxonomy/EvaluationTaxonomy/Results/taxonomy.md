# Evaluation Reason Taxonomy

## Pass Taxonomy (3 categories)

### Faithful Execution (58 entries)
The AI accurately interpreted and implemented the user's explicit instructions.

### Design Improvement (54 entries)
The AI enhanced the UI's usability, clarity, hierarchy, or aesthetics, often demonstrating good design judgment.

### Contextual Preservation (35 entries)
The AI maintained consistency with the existing design system, style, or layout, and avoided making unnecessary or disruptive changes.

## Fail Taxonomy (2 categories)

### Instruction Following Failure (36 entries)
The revision fails to accurately or completely execute the user's explicit instructions.

### Negative Design Impact (39 entries)
The revision introduces a new visual, usability, or functional problem, even if it attempts to follow instructions.
