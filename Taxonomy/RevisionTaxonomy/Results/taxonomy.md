# Revision Task Taxonomy

**7 categories** across **53 unique examples**

## Categories

### Reorganize Information Hierarchy (24 examples)
Changes the high-level structure, positioning, or flow of UI content and components to better guide user attention.

### Refine Layout & Spacing (14 examples)
Adjusts the low-level alignment, spacing, sizing, or composition of UI elements for better visual balance and structure.

### Clarify Function & State (17 examples)
Makes an element's purpose, interactive potential, or current state unambiguous.

### Add or Surface Functionality (4 examples)
Introduces a new capability, control, or piece of information, or makes an existing one more discoverable.

### Simplify & Reduce Clutter (9 examples)
Decreases cognitive load by removing unnecessary elements, consolidating controls, or reducing information density.

### Strengthen Visual Consistency (8 examples)
Aligns the styling, terminology, or interaction patterns of components with other parts of the UI or established conventions.

### Improve Readability & Accessibility (4 examples)
Enhances legibility and contrast to make content easier to perceive and understand, often to meet accessibility standards.
